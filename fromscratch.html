    3 Fun Facts

    1. Originally, water polo was a form of rugby played in rivers and lakes in England and Scotland before it developed over time into what it is today.
    2. Ernest Hemingway played water polo in his early years.
    3. The average water polo player swims 1,600 meters in a game.
      • This is over 17 football lengths worth of swimming!
      • Consequently, water polo players are in fantastic shape.
